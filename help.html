FAQs

  • What is WarnerBros.com?
    WarnerBros.com is your ultimate destination for everything Warner Bros. - from movies and television to home entertainment, games, and more!
  • How do I get tickets to attend a live taping of a show?
    For tickets to attend a live taping of your favorite show, visit www.tvtickets.com for all the information you need. For tickets to the Ellen Degeneres show, go to send.ellentv.com/tickets/.
  • Can I attend a movie premiere?
    Movie premieres are usually by special invitation only.
  • How do I purchase tickets for the Warner Bros. Studio Tour?
    To experience the Warner Bros. Studio Tour, including behind-the-scenes access to your favorite movies and TV shows, visit the Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood or Warner Bros. Studio Tour London website to plan your visit and purchase tickets.
