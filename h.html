FAQs

  • What is WarnerBros.com?
    WarnerBros.com is your one-stop site for everything Warner Bros. - movies, television, home entertainment, games and more!
  • How do I get tickets to attend a live taping of a show?
    If you are interested in attending a live taping of your favorite show, your best bet would be to visit www.tvtickets.com where you will find all the information you need. For tickets to the Ellen Degeneres show, visit http://send.ellentv.com/tickets/.
  • Can I attend a movie premiere?
    Movie premieres are generally through special invitation only.
  • How do I purchase tickets for the Warner Bros. Studio Tour?
    You can visit Warner Bros. Studios! To learn about the tour experience, plan your visit and purchase tickets, visit the Warner Bros. Studio Tour Hollywood or Warner Bros. Studio Tour London website.
  • How do I sign up for exclusive content from WB Games?
    Join WBPlay to gain access to bonus content, participate in the WB Games community, unlock great interactive content and receive WB Games news.

    For a list of available opportunities at our company visit Warner Bros. Careers.
